Not Common Camas

Director, Cinematographer: Ruoyao Ling - Aira

It was a quite random and a bit abrupt desicion to film without a script.
The idea of how "the girl explores the nature and carries a portion of the spirit of nature (symbolised by the camas flower) back into the world of modernness and bustle" was in my mind.
More of this video was meant to appreciate the beauty I bumped into.
