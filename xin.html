PERFORMANCE WRITING FILM ELSE INFO & CONTACT
信(Life Inside Letters)
2025.12.24



Director，Screenwriter, Editor: Ruoyao Ling - Aira
Cinematographer: Ziyang Bao

I came up with this narrative of the experience of writing letters when I am asked to make a one-minute video about myself.
It was fun looking back to my past, those that were unfolded in letters and especially the friendship that me and my friend shared.
All the writing, planning and actual filming was completed in two days.
