A Step
2025.01.23



Director, Screenwriter, Editor: Ruoyao Ling - Aira
Cinematographer: Matthew Shi & Abby Quirt

It could be said that "A Step" was my first actual touch with filmmaking - the whole thing that starts from a solid idea to exporting a "film" from an editing software.
It was adapted from my real experience after starting my life in Canada. The film didn't change the silence and awkwardness until after it was presented in our school's theater.
After watching it with my fist clenched and toes gripping the floor, I voluntarily went onto the stage to join the improv in the following drama class, for the first time in my life.
I forgot what I had played in that section of "freeze", all I remembered was the steady and peaceful pace of my steps on the way back to my seat.
I wasn't feeling excited or proud. I guess I was just more relaxed, like feeling the frozenness in my body quietly melted away.
I am still not a big fan of improv games.
